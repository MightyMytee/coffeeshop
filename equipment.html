Coffee Equipment

Espresso Machine

High-quality espresso machine for professional brewing.

Usage Tips: Follow the manufacturer's instructions for optimal results.

French Press

Classic French press for rich and full-bodied coffee.

Usage Tips: Use coarse ground coffee and steep for 4 minutes.

Pour Over Dripper (V60)

For precise and controlled pour-over brewing, highlighting nuanced flavors.

Usage Tips: Use a gooseneck kettle for even water flow and a medium-fine grind.

Moka Pot

Stovetop espresso maker for strong, concentrated coffee.

Usage Tips: Fill the bottom chamber with hot water to the valve and use a medium-fine grind.

Aeropress

Compact and versatile brewer, known for its clean, smooth coffee.

Usage Tips: Experiment with different grind sizes and brew times for varying results.

Cold Brew Maker

For smooth, low-acid cold brew coffee, perfect for hot days.

Usage Tips: Steep coarse ground coffee in cold water for 12-24 hours.
