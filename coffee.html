Coffee Selection

Ethiopian Yirgacheffe

A bright and floral coffee with notes of lemon and jasmine.

Flavor Notes: Lemon, jasmine, bergamot.

Brewing Recommendation: Pour-over, Aeropress.

Colombian Supremo

A well-balanced coffee with a smooth, nutty flavor and a hint of chocolate.

Flavor Notes: Nutty, chocolate, caramel.

Brewing Recommendation: Drip, French press.

Sumatra Mandheling

An earthy and bold coffee with a rich, full body and low acidity.

Flavor Notes: Earthy, dark chocolate, cedar.

Brewing Recommendation: French press, espresso.

Guatemalan Huehuetenango

Grown in the volcanic soils of Huehuetenango, this coffee boasts a full body and a rich, chocolatey flavor.

Chocolate, caramel, spice

Brewing Recommendation: Excellent for French press or pour-over methods.

Kenyan AA

A classic African coffee known for its bright acidity and complex fruit flavors.

Black currant, grapefruit, tomato

Ideal for drip coffee or Aeropress.
